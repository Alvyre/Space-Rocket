The Game

Space-Rocket is a little Java game where you personify an explorer and his crew stucked the Asteroids Belt. Try to avoid a maximum of asteroids with your space-rocket to escape this hell, and secondarily to gain points !

Fortunately your mothership is equiped of powerful capacities ! such as Time slowing, shield invincibility, or even destroying meteors !

The game is playble with a MIDI controller and a Leapmotion, but don't worry you can still play with your keyboard !

LeapMotion

Your ship is built with a future technology of solar system, so you can control your ship with your hand ! Will you be enough reactive to avoid meteors ? If you don't trust in this curious technology, use the directionnal arrows on the keyboard for a conventionnal way to pilot your ship (approved by the Space Marines Community)

The MIDI Controller

Let's play ! the MIDI Controller is a powerful console to help your crew ! Thanks to this wonderful machine you can activate the capacities of your mothership ! But be careful, once used, the capacities of your ship have a cooldown to reload their power ! During this time you are more vulnerable ! If you're "Ship_console3000" is not available or if you shared it with a friend, prefer use the keyboard which works too !
